Is Online coaching for you?

Online coaching with Simple fitness is offered via an app, which will allow you to track your progress, log your workout and watch exercise demonstrations and you will also be able to manage your nutrition.

Messaging

Via the app you will gain the access to a 1-2-1 messaging system. I will be able to respond at any time.

Weekly Check ins

There will weekly check ins. You will provide progress pics, measurements and weigh ins on the app. This will be reviewed and discussed via zoom.

Full tailored workout plan

This will be tailored to your goals experience and lifestyle. This will include video demonstration, showing you exactly how to perform each movement correctly.

Macros tailored to you

Macros (Carbs, Proteins and fats) is set for you and adjusted on a weekly basis to aid in reaching your goals.
