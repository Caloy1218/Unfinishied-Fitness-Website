My Mission

I am John Carlo Saquing aka "Coach-Caloy", I believe that everyone has the ability to reach the body they want, and I can help you achieve your dream of being fit and healthy.

I started training in 2017, I went to the gym for three months and I stopped because I didn't see any good changes in my body. When I was on vacation, I thought that maybe there was something wrong with what I was doing, so there was no change in my body, because the internet was trendy at that time, I used it to find a solution to the mistakes I had made.

I believe my mission in life is to help people become their best self, whether that is to lose weight, gain muscle or get fitter. As my time as a personal trainer I have helped many clients reach their fitness goals. I have adapted my own method which will get you from A to B.

Fitness shouldn’t be complicated, you read a lot of different ways to lose weight and gain muscle and you don’t know where to start! In my coaching, I simplify everything so its easy to follow.

To know more about what I offer check out my online coaching.

¨Fitness is not about being better than someone else. It’s about being better than you used to be.¨
